Forecasting breaks when data is disconnected

Many forecasting tools assume short lead times and stable supply. Import and distribution teams deal with variable lead times, container cycles, and supplier changes. Forecasting must incorporate real inbound visibility and replenishment risk.

If purchasing, inventory, and shipments live in separate tools, your forecast is always late. You only discover risk after stockouts or overstock.

FlowIQ connects these datasets so forecasting is operational, not theoretical.

What to measure

  • Weeks of coverage by SKU and by warehouse.
  • Lead time risk and inbound pipeline visibility.
  • Service level targets (avoid stockouts without excess stock).
  • Margin-aware planning (buy the right SKUs, not just the fast movers).

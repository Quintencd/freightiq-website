Frequently Asked Questions

What happens when I hit my limits?

You'll see a notification at 80% usage with options to upgrade or buy add-ons. At 100%, you'll need to upgrade or purchase add-ons to continue.

Are shipments counted monthly or annually?

Monthly. Your shipment counter resets on the 1st of each month. Growth gets 3 import + 3 export per month (36+36 per year).

Can I upgrade mid-month?

Yes! Upgrades apply immediately and we'll prorate the billing. Your new limits take effect right away.

What's the difference between Baseline and AI Forecasting?

Baseline (Growth): Simple moving average predictions. AI (Professional): Machine learning with p10/p50/p90 scenarios, seasonality, and trend analysis. This is THE upgrade reason from Growth to Professional.

Do you offer annual billing?

Yes! Save 10% on Growth, Professional, and Scale (17% on Starter) by paying annually. Annual pricing shown above.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, monthly plans can be cancelled anytime. Annual plans are non-refundable but you keep access for the full year.

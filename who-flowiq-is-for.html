Who FlowIQ is For

FlowIQ is built for businesses that need real decision intelligence, not just data entry.

FlowIQ is a great fit if you're:

Importers & Distributors

You import goods and need accurate landed cost tracking, FX management, and margin visibility across multiple shipments.

Multi-branch Inventory Businesses

You manage inventory across multiple locations and need forecasting, stock optimization, and transfer management.

Light Manufacturing / Assembly

You assemble or manufacture products and need BOM tracking, component costing, and production planning.

Businesses Using Xero + Spreadsheets

You're managing operations in spreadsheets and Xero, but need better visibility into landed costs, margins, and inventory forecasting.

Businesses Suffering From:

  • Margin leakage from inaccurate cost allocation
  • Over/under-stocking due to poor forecasting
  • FX & landed cost uncertainty affecting pricing decisions

FlowIQ may not be a fit if you're:

Micro Traders

Very small operations with minimal inventory and no need for forecasting or advanced cost tracking.

One-off Importers

Businesses that import occasionally and don't need ongoing inventory management or forecasting.

The manufacturing cost problem

Manufacturers often have accurate shop-floor processes but unreliable cost visibility. Component prices change, freight changes, FX changes, and a small variance per unit becomes a large variance per batch.

When inventory valuation, landed cost, and BOM costing are managed in separate systems (or spreadsheets), your margin reporting drifts. That leads to overconfidence in pricing, late detection of cost creep, and missed reorder timing.

FlowIQ keeps purchasing, inventory, and landed cost in one workflow so you can see true cost and profit in real time.

What FlowIQ helps you control

  • Inventory accuracy across warehouses and production locations.
  • True per-unit cost by capturing landed costs on imported components.
  • BOM and manufacturing visibility (where applicable) without ERP heaviness.
  • Forecast-driven planning to reduce stockouts and overstock.
  • Margin visibility that matches inventory valuation and accounting outcomes.

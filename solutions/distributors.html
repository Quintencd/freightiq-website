Why distributors leak margin

Distribution businesses usually have strong sales execution and weak cost consistency. Supplier costs change, freight changes, and landed cost is applied inconsistently. That creates invisible margin drift until month-end.

On the inventory side, stock is spread across warehouses, transfers are common, and a single spreadsheet mistake cascades into purchasing errors, stockouts, or overstock.

FlowIQ keeps purchasing, inventory, and costing connected so operational decisions reflect true cost and true availability.

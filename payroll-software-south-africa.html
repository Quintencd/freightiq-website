PayrollIQ FAQ

Answers for payroll buyers comparing payroll software, SARS workpapers, and plan availability.

Does PayrollIQ support South African payroll workpapers?

Yes. PayrollIQ supports EMP201 preparation workpapers, IRP5 employee certificate workpapers, and EMP501 reconciliation workpapers for review before final submission.

Can PayrollIQ manage leave and payslips?

Yes. PayrollIQ supports employee leave setup, leave approval routing, leave balances, pay runs, and payslip workflows.

Which FlowIQ plans include PayrollIQ?

PayrollIQ is included on Professional, Scale, and Enterprise. Starter and Growth do not include PayrollIQ.

Does PayrollIQ post payroll into accounting?

Yes. PayrollIQ can post payroll journals into AccountingIQ so payroll accounting entries remain aligned with the finance workflow.

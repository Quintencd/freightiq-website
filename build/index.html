Questions, answered

Straight answers to the most common concerns.

Will FreightIQ replace my ERP?

No. FreightIQ complements your ERP by handling landed cost, forecasting, pricing and logistics automation. We integrate via CSV/API and keep your ERP as the source of truth.

How fast can we go live?

Most teams start in under a week. Import existing SKUs, costs and shipment history, then enable automation module‑by‑module.

Is our data secure?

Yes. Role‑based access, audit logs and encrypted data at rest/in transit. We support SSO on Enterprise.

What integrations do you support?

CSV import/export out of the box. API access on Pro. We commonly connect to accounting, ERP and courier platforms; custom integrations on Enterprise.

Can we cancel anytime?

Yes. Monthly plans with no lock‑in. Your data remains yours; export at any time.

What importers need from inventory software

Import businesses deal with volatility: freight price changes, FX movement, duty changes, and supplier price creep. If inventory software treats stock as a simple quantity and ignores landed cost complexity, valuation and profit reporting drift fast.

That drift turns into mispricing and buying mistakes. Best sellers go out of stock, slow movers pile up, and your reorder logic is no longer anchored to actual lead-time risk.

FlowIQ is built import-first so inventory accuracy includes cost truth, not just stock counts. If you also need a tighter purchasing and shipment control layer, see our import ERP software page.

Key outcomes

  • FIFO-aware stock valuation aligned to real landed cost.
  • Margin visibility by SKU that matches procurement and freight reality.
  • Cleaner purchasing decisions using lead times and demand planning.
  • Auditable history across shipments, costs, receipts, and adjustments.

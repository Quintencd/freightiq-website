What importers need from inventory software

Import businesses are exposed to volatility: freight prices, FX movement, duty changes, and supplier price creep. If inventory software treats stock as a simple quantity and ignores landed cost complexity, your valuation and profit reporting drift.

That drift shows up as mispricing and buying mistakes. You end up with stockouts on best sellers, and overstock on slow movers because your reorder logic is not anchored to lead-time reality.

FlowIQ is built import-first so inventory accuracy includes cost truth, not just stock counts.

Key outcomes

  • FIFO-aware stock valuation aligned to real landed cost.
  • Margin visibility by SKU that matches your procurement and freight reality.
  • Cleaner purchasing decisions with lead times and demand planning.
  • Auditable history: shipments, costs, receipts, and adjustments.

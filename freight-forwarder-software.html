The forwarding operations problem

Freight forwarders win on execution. But execution creates complexity: multiple invoices per job, split shipments, partial deliveries, carrier delays, and constant client queries. If job costing and status updates live in separate tools, margin accuracy and client experience both suffer.

FlowIQ reduces operational noise with one view for job costs, documents, shipment movement, inventory impact, and update-ready status information.

What you can measure and improve

  • Job profitability and cost completeness.
  • Freight allocation into true landed cost outcomes.
  • Cycle time per leg and per carrier.
  • Exceptions and rework causes such as missing documents or late clearance.
  • Client communication consistency from one shared data story.

The forwarding operations problem

Freight forwarders win on execution. But execution creates complexity: multiple invoices per job, split shipments, partial deliveries, carrier delays, and constant client queries. If job costing and status updates live in separate tools, margin accuracy and client experience both suffer.

Most teams end up with a spreadsheet for job profitability, a WhatsApp chain for updates, and an inbox of exceptions. That works until volume increases.

FlowIQ is designed to reduce operational noise: one view for job costs, documents, and update-ready status information.

What you can measure and improve

  • Job profitability and cost completeness (did we capture every charge?).
  • Freight allocation to understand true landed cost outcomes.
  • Cycle time per leg and per carrier.
  • Exceptions and rework causes (documents missing, late clearance, etc.).
  • Client communication consistency (same data, fewer manual updates).
